## Overview

The NOA Study Abroad Assistant is a conversational chatbot built with Python and Rasa, aimed at guiding international students through the admissions process. It provides accurate responses to frequently asked questions and facilitates a smoother transition for new students.

## Key Features

- **Conversational Interface:** Enables interactive, natural language queries.
- **Admissions Guidance:** Provides detailed information on study abroad and admission processes.
- **Customizable Dialogues:** Trained on common student inquiries to deliver precise answers.

## Challenges and Learnings

Designing a chatbot that reliably understands and processes varied student queries was challenging. I focused on fine-tuning dialogue management and training the model on diverse datasets to ensure accurate and helpful interactions.

## Outcome

The RASA Chatbot effectively assists international students by simplifying access to admissions information, enhancing the overall study abroad support experience.
